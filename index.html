Iterate

As a writer myself, motivation is often lacking. Introducing Iterate, which helps you build-up a writing habit! When you meet your word count, you gain a streak. Streaks reset after a day. It lets you know based on your habit how close you are to finishing a traditional novel (80k words). I used this to help write my first novel, Kingmaker, available on Amazon

Spaced Repetition

Spaced Repetition helps you learn a foreign language with flash cards. This application was practice in traversing a linked-list data structure and test-driven development. All the tests were first written in Cypress and then the rest of the application was developed around them. The cards are generated via a linked-list data structure.

Pokemon Go! Raid Organizer Bot for Discord

A bot made for an independent client, which organizes your Pokemon Go! raids by users reacting to an emoji on your invite. Once the raid if full, you receive a DM in a game-friendly format to copy and paste your all invitations in one go. Currently, the bot is hosted by the client so cannot be invited to your server at this time. However, the source code is available.

PopeBot

A very custom Discord Bot for maintaining humor and purity in a Catholic Discord server. Featuring an image of Saint John Paul II, PopeBot features many inside jokes that has kept members entertained for nearly a year. In addition to being funny, it has handy features such as keeping up with the Church Liturgical Calendar, Feast Days, and the Catechism.

I always try to put some fun into what I do. Feel free to mention any personal touches in your Discord Bot requests!

Please contact me directly if you wish this bot to join your server.
